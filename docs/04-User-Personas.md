# Purpose

This document defines the primary users of JobAgentAI.

Understanding the needs, goals, and challenges of each user persona helps ensure that product decisions are based on real user problems rather than assumptions.

Every major feature should provide value to at least one defined persona.

# Persona 1 – Fresh Graduate

## Persona Summary

A recent college graduate or entry-level job seeker who is actively looking for their first full-time professional role.

This persona has limited job application experience and often needs guidance throughout the application process.

---

## Background

- Recently graduated from college or university.
- Has little or no professional work experience.
- May have internship, volunteer, or academic project experience.
- Applies to multiple entry-level positions across different job platforms.
- Often uncertain about resume writing, cover letters, and interview preparation.

---

## Goals

- Find a suitable entry-level job as quickly as possible.
- Create a professional resume that stands out.
- Write effective cover letters with minimal experience.
- Stay organized while applying to multiple companies.
- Prepare confidently for interviews.
- Track the progress of each application.

---

## Pain Points

- Unsure how to create an effective resume.
- Has little or no interview experience.
- Repeatedly enters the same information on different job platforms.
- Struggles to keep track of submitted applications.
- Doesn't know which jobs best match their skills.
- Feels overwhelmed by using multiple job websites.
- Often forgets follow-ups or application deadlines.

---

## Current Workflow

1. Search for jobs on multiple platforms (e.g., LinkedIn, JobStreet, Indeed).
2. Open each job posting individually to review the requirements.
3. Update or customize their resume manually.
4. Write or modify a cover letter for each application.
5. Complete application forms by repeatedly entering the same personal information.
6. Track applications using spreadsheets, notes, or memory.
7. Check email regularly for interview invitations or updates.
8. Prepare for interviews using online resources.

---

## How JobAgentAI Helps

JobAgentAI simplifies the job application journey by providing a centralized platform where users can:

- Discover jobs from multiple job platforms in one place.
- Organize and track every application.
- Manage multiple resumes for different roles.
- Generate AI-assisted cover letters.
- Receive AI-powered job match insights.
- Prepare for interviews with AI assistance.
- Automate repetitive application tasks in future releases while keeping the user in control.

---

## Technical Skill Level

**Intermediate**

The user is comfortable using common web applications such as job boards, email, and document editors but has limited experience with advanced software tools or automation.


---

## Preferred Job Platforms

- LinkedIn
- JobStreet
- Indeed
- Company Career Pages
- University Career Portals

---

## Success Criteria

The Fresh Graduate considers JobAgentAI successful if they can:

- Find relevant entry-level opportunities more efficiently.
- Stay organized throughout the application process.
- Feel more confident when applying for jobs.
- Reduce repetitive work when submitting applications.
- Increase their chances of receiving interview invitations.

---

## Phase 1 Features That Help This Persona

- Unified Job Discovery
- Resume Management
- Application Tracker
- Dashboard
- AI Match Score
- AI Cover Letter Generation
- Export Application History

---

## User Quote

"I just want a simple way to keep track of all my applications and know I'm applying to the right jobs."

---

## Opportunities

Potential opportunities to improve this user's experience:

- AI Resume Builder
- AI Interview Coach
- AI Resume Feedback
- Smart Job Recommendations
- Interview Preparation Checklist

# Persona 2 – Active Job Seeker

## Persona Summary

An experienced professional who is actively searching for new career opportunities while managing multiple job applications across different platforms.

This persona values efficiency, organization, and tools that reduce repetitive work without sacrificing control over the application process.

## Background

- Has professional work experience (typically 2+ years, but may vary).
- Frequently applies to multiple companies at the same time.
- Maintains different resume versions for different roles.
- Uses several job platforms to maximize opportunities.
- Often balances job searching while still employed.

## Persona Priority

**Primary Persona**

The initial MVP should prioritize solving the needs of this persona. Product decisions should be evaluated based on whether they improve the experience for Active Job Seekers.
