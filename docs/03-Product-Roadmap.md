# Purpose

This document defines the planned evolution of JobAgentAI. It outlines the major product phases, key capabilities, and priorities that guide development from the Minimum Viable Product (MVP) to future releases.

The roadmap is intended to provide direction for planning, prioritization, and long-term product growth.

---

# Product Philosophy

JobAgentAI is designed to simplify and streamline the entire job application journey.

Instead of becoming another job board, JobAgentAI serves as a centralized platform that helps users discover opportunities, organize applications, prepare application materials, and automate repetitive tasks while keeping users in control.

Every roadmap phase should deliver meaningful value independently while building toward the long-term vision of an AI-powered job application assistant.

---

# Product Vision Statement

JobAgentAI aims to become the central platform where job seekers manage every stage of their career application journey.

Rather than replacing existing job platforms, JobAgentAI integrates with them to provide a unified experience for discovering opportunities, organizing applications, leveraging AI assistance, and automating repetitive tasks while keeping users in control.

The long-term vision is to become an intelligent career companion that helps users find better opportunities with less effort.

---

# Product Principles

1. User remains in control.
2. AI assists, not replaces, decision making.
3. Automate repetitive work, not important decisions.
4. Integrate with existing job platforms rather than competing with them.
5. Build trust before increasing automation.
